- turn your favorite recipes into a grocery list in a sec -



How to Recipe2Go?


Step 1: Use the search bar to find a recipe of any cusine! Simply type in any keywords or ingredients.

Step 2: Select desired recipe from the results. This will bring you to a separate page with all of the listed ingredients!

Step 3: Import the list of ingredients to Walmart shopping cart as you like. Here, you are all ready to go and ready to make some delicious meals!
