How to Recipe2Go?


Step 1: Use the search bar to find a recipe of any cusine! Simply type in any keywords or ingredients.

Step 2: Select desired recipe from the results. Add or take out any unwanted ingredients to make the recipe just the way you like!

Step 3: Import the list of ingredients of the modified recipe to a grocery list. Here, you are all ready to go! We can even add those to the shopping cart for you from Walmart!
